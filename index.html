Welcome to Armatillo!

A habit reversal tracker

Track and break unwanted body-focused repetitive behaviors with our specialized app.

Learn More

Identify triggers that lead to habit engagement

Armatillo helps you recognize patterns and situations that trigger your body-focused repetitive behaviors. By understanding your triggers, you can develop effective strategies to break the habit cycle.

Create strategies for the reversal process

Develop personalized strategies to help you break unwanted habits. Armatillo guides you through creating competing responses and stimulus controls tailored to your specific needs.

Instead of engaging in your habit, use a strategy instead!

When you feel the urge to engage in your habit, Armatillo helps you implement alternative strategies. Access your personalized competing responses and stimulus controls to redirect your behavior.

Control your stimuli or use a competing response

Armatillo provides tools to manage environmental triggers and develop competing responses. Create a supportive environment that reduces urges and promotes healthier behavior patterns.

Track the habit you wish to reverse!

Monitor your progress with Armatillo's comprehensive tracking tools. Record when habits occur, identify patterns, and see your improvement over time.

Dill, the armadillo, will react to your progress and encourage you along the way!

Meet Dill, your supportive companion throughout your habit reversal journey. Dill celebrates your successes and provides encouragement when you need it most.
